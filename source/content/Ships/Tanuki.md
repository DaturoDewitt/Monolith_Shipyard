It carries a healthy amount of laser firepower for its size, sporting 2 Prometheus Laser Cannons for long-range effective fire and 3 Cerberus Plasma Scattercannons for melting hostiles in close range. Houses a small galley and medbay, standard for militia ships, and is FTL capable.|
